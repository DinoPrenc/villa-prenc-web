Location

Villa Prenc is set in Matulji, Šmogorska cesta 13a.
It is 5 km from Opatija and 15 km from Rijeka. Nearest beach is 3km and nearest airport is 40 km away from the house

About

Villa Prenc is a modern, newly built house in the center of Matulji, 3km from the nearest beach. It provides three bedrooms with own bathrooms, fully equiped kitchen, pool, big garden with children playground, table tennis, outdoor grill, a tavern with a wine collection and much more. We can accommodate up to 8 guests


At your disposal you have four beautiful terraces for relax and enjoy the beautiful sunset with a glass of good wine. If you wish for summer bustle and beautiful beaches, we suggest visiting Opatija, famous for its architecture and beautiful parks. For all nature lovers, there is Ucka Nature Park with numerous mountain and bike trails. Also Istria is nearby for more interesting adventures.
